Co-design approaches and tools in service design projects

The thesis is a research exploring Service Design and Co- design methodologies trough the observation of the redesign of the homeless shelter services in Turin, a project by Costruire Bellezza. The first part of the research is an introduction to the two methods exploring the features, methodology and benefits they bring, analyzing the actors that can be involved, the tools that can be used and the outcome they can generate. The second part of the thesis is a critical observation of the project of Costruire Bellezza. The thesis visualize and analyze the human centered approach and the tools utilized for establishing linkages with different actors in the process and generate ideas for the definition of the new system.
