Biak

The project was commissioned by the municipality of Bilbao to redesign and semplificate the OTA machine (parking kiosks) from its design to its interface. During the research phase we observed firsts impressions of the users interacting with OTA machine and how the users engage with the service. We did some interviews to the people using the service, asking what were their feeling using this machine, if it was clear the process, what were the pain points,... The interface has been redesigned from zero. We wanted to make clearer the system of zones (blue and orange) and how to pay for the time spent in the parking lot. Also the panel of the instruction has been redesigned. We decided to use a touch screen interface for our redesign. This would free up the cluttered face of the machine, only displaying the relevant buttons when they were needed. The user would be able to easily take the correct action without interference.
