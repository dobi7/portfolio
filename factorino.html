FACTORINO

Factoryno represents Turin creative system not denying the distinctive industrial soul of the city but adding to it the city artistic energy. From this kind of union comes "the creative machine." Just three colors, white and grey recalling the industry itself and yellow to add movement and contrast are joined with simple lines, inspired by electronic circuits that become a path. The advertising campaign proposes various elements: posters, flyers, gadgets (t-shirts, cards, bags), a social campaign (based on Instagram filters), an app, a website and a video-teaser.
